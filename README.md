# 🚀 CS Risk Radar AI

## 📌 Problem
Customer service teams often react too late when customers are already frustrated.  
There is no system to detect escalation risk early.

---

## 💡 Solution
CS Risk Radar is an AI system that analyzes customer conversations in real-time to:
- Detect escalation risk
- Identify frustration signals (repetition, negative tone)
- Provide actionable insights
- Recommend next steps for CS teams

---

## 🧠 Key Features
- 🔴 Risk Level Detection (Low → Critical)
- 🎯 Risk Score & Trend
- ⚠️ Trigger Signal Detection
- 🧠 Customer Insight
- 💡 Operational Risk Analysis
- ✅ Recommended Actions
- 🚀 AI-generated Next Actions:
  - Recovery message
  - Escalation brief
  - Audit checklist
