# CS Risk Radar AI

## Problem
Customer service teams cannot detect escalation risk early.

## Solution
AI analyzes conversations to detect risk, identify operational issues, and suggest next actions.

## Key Features
- Risk Level detection
- Trigger signal identification
- Customer & operational insight
- Recommended actions
- AI-generated recovery message & escalation brief
